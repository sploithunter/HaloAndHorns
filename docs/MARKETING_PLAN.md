# Marketing Plan — Pet Realm (Halo & Horns)

**Objective:** convert a 50,000 Robux ad budget into 500+ highly-engaged players
(the all-ages reach unlock + fee refund), while building the creative assets and
funnel data to scale spend efficiently once the under-13 market opens.

**The one strategic fact:** until the reach gate opens, ads can only convert
**16+ users**. Plan accordingly — creative for this phase should lean *game depth*
(combat, builds, trading, endgame chase) over *cuteness*, then re-test cute-forward
creative when all-ages unlocks and the native pet-sim audience arrives.

---

## 1. Positioning

**Pitch (internal):** a pet sim where the pets actually fight — City-of-Heroes
combat bones under a hatch-and-collect economy, with a heaven/hell choice that
changes the rules of the world.

**Hooks, ranked by 16+ appeal:**
1. **Your pets are your raid team.** Tanks pull, healers heal, holds mez — real
   aggro, real builds, 10-pet squads. No other pet sim has this.
2. **Heaven or Hell — pick a direction.** Ascend and coexist; descend and fight
   everything. Deep hell is literally dark; you bring a lantern pet.
3. **The Trials.** Procedurally-generated dungeon runs — everyone's Trial #N is
   the same map, so "Trial 28 was great" is a thing you can say to a friend.
4. **The chase.** Boss-drop exclusive eggs (0.5%), Platinum century chains
   (100 trials + level 50), secrets at 1-in-4000, tradeable everything.
5. **Duo everything.** Shared kill credit, shared chest loot, team mining bonus,
   sidekick leveling — built to be played with a friend.

**Competitor frame:** Pet Simulator 99 / BGSI own "numbers go up + cute."
We don't out-cute them at this stage — we out-*game* them. "The pet sim for
people who think pet sims are shallow" is the 16+ wedge.

## 2. Creative: what to make + prompts for testing visuals

Roblox ads live or die on the ICON (sponsored tile) and THUMBNAILS (page
conversion). Test 4–6 icons and 3–4 thumbnails minimum. Rules of thumb: one
readable subject, extreme color contrast against Roblox's dark UI, faces/eyes
outperform scenes, ≤3 words of text on thumbnails, none on icons.

### Icon test matrix (generate 2–3 variants each)

Prompts written for an AI image tool; render at 1024×1024, square, no text.
House style anchor: vibrant stylized low-poly, saturated, rim-lit.

- **ICON-A "The Duality"** — `Split-composition game icon: left half radiant
  golden heaven with a white winged fawn pet, right half ember-lit hell with a
  black horned hound pet, both pets facing camera with big expressive eyes,
  vibrant stylized low-poly 3D render, strong rim lighting, saturated colors,
  square game icon, no text`
- **ICON-B "The Lantern"** — `A small glowing white dove pet radiating warm

  cinematic lighting, square game icon, no text`
- **ICON-C "The Squad"** — `A determined low-poly adventurer flanked by five

## 3. The 50,000 Robux test plan

**Prerequisites (do BEFORE spending anything):**
- [ ] Icon + thumbnail set uploaded (from §2 tests)
- [ ] First-90-seconds pass: spawn → see an egg, a fight, and the portal row
      without walking more than 30s; tutorial doesn't block the fun
- [ ] Analytics baseline recorded: current D1, avg session, engaged count
- [ ] Reach questionnaire resubmitted (No/No) — clean descriptors
- [ ] Ideally: builder Phase 1 (Home art) shipped — if not, accept a retention
      handicap and budget accordingly

**Phase 1 — Creative screening (6,000 R, ~week 1)**
- 3 flights × 2,000 R, one icon each (best guesses: A, B, E), same targeting.
- Run Thu–Sun to overlap Warpath Thursday / Frenzy Friday / Wyrm Weekend —
  the generous days make the best first sessions.
- **Measure:** CTR (tile→visit) and cost-per-play per icon.
- **Gate:** kill any icon >2× the CPP of the best. Winner advances.

**Phase 2 — Funnel validation (10,000 R, ~week 2)**
- One flight, winning icon, thumbnails ordered by §2 matrix.
- **Measure:** D1 return, avg session length, and the engaged-players slope
  (the 0→500 graph on the Audience Reach page — check every 2 days).
- **Gate:** compute engaged-per-1,000-Robux. If the run-rate projects 500
  engaged within the remaining budget ±50%, scale. If not, STOP SPENDING —
  every remaining Robux goes to fixing retention (map, onramp), not media.

**Phase 3 — Scale (28,000 R, weeks 3–5)**
- Winning creative, spend weighted Thu–Sun, refresh the thumbnail once
  mid-flight (creative fatigue is real at small audience sizes).
- Watch the engaged counter weekly; expected crossing: 500 before budget out
  if Phase 2 gate passed honestly.

**Reserve (6,000 R):** re-screening one new creative mid-Phase-3, or a
celebration flight the week all-ages unlocks (the audience 10×es that day —
be visible in it).

**KPI cheat sheet:** healthy = CTR ≥ 1%, CPP ≤ 5 R, D1 ≥ 15%, session ≥ 12 min,
engaged conversion ≥ 4% of unique players. The engaged slope is the only KPI
that pays money back (fee refund + all-ages unlock).

**Free channels in parallel (zero Robux, feeds the same counter):**
- TikTok/Shorts/YouTube of the §2 video + hatch moments + "my pets cleared
  this dungeon" clips — pet content travels organically.
- Roblox groups/Discord: Frenzy Friday and Wyrm Weekend are recurring
  community beats — announce them; recurring events are retention machinery.
- The Star Code / creator collab lane once all-ages opens.

---

## 4. After the unlock

When the 500 crosses: resubmit reach, confirm all-ages, then RE-RUN the §2
creative screen against the youth audience — expect the cute-forward icons
(B "Lantern", E "Huge") to invert the rankings, and CPP to drop substantially.
The 50k playbook re-runs at that point with better unit economics and whatever
map the builder has shipped. That's the real launch; this budget buys the door.
